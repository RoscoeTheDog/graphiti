## Sprint Goal

Implement automatic reconciliation of remediation test results with blocked validation stories. When a remediation story fixes test failures, the system will automatically propagate results to blocked validation stories (propagate mode), unblock for retesting (retest mode), or mark as superseded (supersede mode), eliminating redundant re-testing and manual intervention.
